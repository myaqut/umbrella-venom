Safety & Regulatory

Safety Considerations for Buyers

These points must be communicated to end-users and should be included in B2B supply contracts.

⚠️
Allergy Risk
PLA2 is the primary allergen. 1–5% of the global population may be allergic. Mandatory disclosure to all downstream buyers and end-users.
🚫
Contraindications
Not for use during pregnancy, active cancer, infections, post-vaccination, Type 1 diabetes, or in children under 5 years of age.
💉
Allergy Test Required
All therapeutic use must be preceded by a bee venom allergy test. This requirement should be explicitly stated in all B2B contracts.
✅
Wide Therapeutic Safety Margin
Whole BV becomes toxic only at 200–500× the therapeutic dose — significantly safer than isolated components used in isolation.
❄️
Storage Requirements
Store in cool, dry, dark conditions at 0°C. Dry powder form is inherently stable — avoid moisture contamination at all times.
